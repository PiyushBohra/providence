We are an Authorized Partner
Redis is an open-source, in-memory data store used by unparallel clients for their solutions to augmented as either a database, streaming engine, caching or a message broker. The applications of Redis is not limited to any one cloud but with its capability has partnership and integration capabilities with all major cloud providers namely Microsoft Azure, Amazon Web Services or Google Cloud Platform.
Prodevans has its expertise with Redis and being a partner we have implemented solutions right from as using Redis as a centralized cache for your enterprise solutions but also its use cases as a caching or streaming database service as per business use cases of our customers. Not limited to where we have also helped our customers to tap its Enterprise level capabilities with applications in tandem with either a virtual machine, Kubernetes or a hybrid cloud. Our team of developers are always evolving with use cases of Redis to deliver best possible outcomes with respect to costs, performance and scalable solutions.
