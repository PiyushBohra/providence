Our Expertise
We focus on Cloud Native Application Development, Infrastructure As code , DevSecOps, Containers and Container Orchestration, Virtualization and Cloud Technologies , Infrastructure Automation
1.
Cloud services
Prodevans specializes in delivering comprehensive cloud services on both public and private clouds across D0, D1. D2 ops. We are partners to multiple OEMS . With extensive expertise in VMware, OpenStack, and Nutanix we can help you irrespective of the technology choices. We have delivered solutions based on various orchestration / management planes around multiple compute, storage and network technologies
2.
Application Modernisation
Organisations are embracing cloud technologies to enhance cost-effectiveness and boost returns on investment. As part of this process, they are adopting containerisation and migrating legacy applications. However, many companies face challenges due to skill shortages and the evolving nature of Kubernetes and Docker. Prodevans offers its expertise and experience in Open Source technologies to help organisations successfully navigate this journey and become self-sufficient in container adoption.
3.
360 Degree Monitoring
Prodevans offers comprehensive 360-degree monitoring solutions for applications and infrastructure, ensuring optimal performance and reliability. As partners with ELK (Elasticsearch, Logstash, Kibana), we provide robust log management and analytics capabilities to gain valuable insights into system behavior. We are also well versed with native monitoring stacks of leading public cloud providers, such as AWS CloudWatch and Azure Monitor & sentinel and other OEM tools such as MicroFocus SIEM, ManageEngine, Dynatrace, Splunk, DataDog.
4.
Identity & Access Management
Prodevans in house developed Identity and Access Management solution ZTrust offers a simple, secure and reliable way to deliver a single sign-on experience for your customers— as well as providing peace of mind for your IT team. Customers can move seamlessly through a transaction with your organisation, without needing to login to other applications.
