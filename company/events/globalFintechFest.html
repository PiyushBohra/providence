About the event
Prodevans Technologies is at the forefront of integrating AI with financial infrastructures, offering cutting-edge solutions like ZTrust, LivestreamIQ, QweryAI, and CIMS.

These tools are designed to:
• Promote Innovation: By providing advanced AI-driven insights and automation.
• Enhance Efficiency: Through intelligent monitoring and management systems.
• Strengthen Security: With AI-based compliance and risk management solutions.
• Support Inclusivity: By offering scalable, accessible, and user-friendly tools.
• Ensure Resilience: With solutions that enable financial institutions to adapt and thrive in a rapidly changing environment.

As we look ahead to the next decade of finance, the role of technology, especially Artificial Intelligence (AI), will be pivotal in shaping a future that is innovative, secure, and inclusive. The Global Fintech Fest 2024 centers on three crucial pillars: Responsible AI, Inclusivity, and Resilience—each of which aligns perfectly with Prodevans Technologies’ mission to revolutionize financial infrastructures.

At Global Fintech Fest 2024, Prodevans Technologies invites you to explore these solutions and see how they can transform your financial operations. Visit us at Booth YA11, Hall Jasmine 1, and discover how Prodevans is leading the charge in fintech innovation.
Offering Solutions
ZTrust
  • A secure and simplified access management solution that offers Single Sign-On (SSO), multifactor authentication, and centralized user access management. It enhances security by blocking suspicious logins and enforcing strong password policies. ZTrust is designed to integrate seamlessly with existing authentication systems and is compliant with ISO 27001, GDPR, and WCAG 2.0 standards.
LivestreamIQ
  • An intelligent Kafka management tool that provides real-time monitoring, proactive alerting, and performance optimization. LivestreamIQ simplifies Kafka infrastructure management with features like dynamic topic configuration, multi-cluster management, and unified notification gateways, ensuring high availability and security.
QweryAI
  • An AI-powered enterprise search and knowledge management tool that leverages GPT technology to enhance information retrieval and workflow efficiency. QweryAI enables organizations to streamline operations, reduce information overload, and improve employee productivity through contextual and conversational searches.
CIMS
  • A regulatory reporting solution designed for the financial sector, particularly in compliance with RBI requirements. CIMS automates data collection, report generation, and validation processes, providing advanced analytics and ensuring data accuracy and transparency.
