LIFE AT PRODEVANS

We foster a culture of innovation and continuous learning and just recognition

Our dedicated teams are at the forefront of building cutting-edge cloud and container platforms, working together to solve complex challenges and deliver exceptional solutions. With a strong emphasis on work-life balance and professional growth, Prodevans values its employees' well-being and provides ample opportunities for career development and advancement in the dynamic realm of cloud and DevSecOps.90% of our staff are certified on more than two technologies. 80% add a certification each year.Examinations are still a norm. No we are not a school, we party and you are welcome.

Reward & Recognition
We value and appreciate the sincere efforts and dedication put in by our people. At Prodevans, good commitment and performance are always recognised and rewarded.
Health Insurance
Our health insurance policy covers employees, their spouses and children.During COVID-19 we helped all our employees to reimburse all expenses including tests as well as vaccinations.
Wellness Program
We nurture wellness programs that help our employees cope with anxiety, physical & mental stress. Some of these include offering flexible work schedules, healthy food offerings, maternity & paternity leave etc.
Hybrid WorkModel
